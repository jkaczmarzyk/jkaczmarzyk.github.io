Biography

Hi, my name is Justyna and I graduated with my Masters in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC) in spring of 2022. My research interests include machine learning, natural language processing (NLP) and causal inference. After graduating I spent the summer working as a teacher with Girls Who Code, fostering interest in computer science amongst a group of high school students. Soon after, I began working as a Machine Learning Engineer at Prudential.

For fun, I enjoy creative coding and am interested in applying computer programming to the process of creating art and music.
